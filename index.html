Ask Margaret

A friendly button on your screen. Tap, speak, get help.

Ask Margaret is a small desktop helper. Press the blue button, say what you need, and Margaret shows you exactly where to click.

1 Get your free Gemini key

Ask Margaret uses Google's free Gemini AI to understand your question. You just need a key — a short secret password from Google. It is free.

  1. Click this link: https://aistudio.google.com/apikey
  2. Sign in with your normal Google account (the one you use for Gmail).
  3. Click the blue button that says Create API key.
  4. A long string of letters and numbers will appear. It starts with AIza. Copy it (right-click → Copy, or press Ctrl+C).
  5. Keep it somewhere safe. You will paste it into Ask Margaret in step 3.

2 Download & install Ask Margaret

Click the big button below to download the installer. Then double-click the downloaded file and follow the prompts. Windows may warn you that the app is from an unknown publisher — click More info → Run anyway.

3 Paste your key when the app opens

The first time you run Ask Margaret, a welcome window will ask for the key you copied in step 1. Paste it into the box and click Save and start. You only do this once.

4 That's it — use Margaret

A small blue Help button will sit in the corner of your screen. Whenever you're stuck, click it, say your question out loud, and Margaret will draw a circle around where to click.
